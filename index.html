Now that I'm back, I'm looking forward to grow in the software world and hopefully I can also utilize my knowledge of micro-controllers and circuits that I got at Collège Boréal as well.

The summer is one of my most active times in the year with all my adventures, the fall is for hunting and winter is for snowboarding.

📚 I discovered my love for books during my time in Ottawa. Living alone in the full force of the pandemic, I found new ways to keep myself busy. 📝 I'm currently trying to level up my JavaScript skills. I'm reading Eloquent Javascript by Marijn Haverbeke and I'm hoping it will elevate my current knowledge of the language. I'm looking at starting to learn React and Redux as well. I wanted to expand my front end library experience and then compare Vue with React to see which one I like best in the end.
