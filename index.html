Biography

👋 Hey! My name is Vincent Préseault, I'm currently in my first year of Electronics Engineering Technology at Collège Boréal. I'm looking to do the 3 year program with specialization in instrumentation and electronics.

🌲 Growing up in northern Ontario, I quickly got into hunting and fishing. I also love to go car camping on the weekend taking advantage of all the hiking trails in the area. The summer is one of my most active times in the year with all my adventures, the fall is for hunting and winter is for snowboarding.

💻 Prior to my time at Boréal, I studied Software Engineering at the University of Ottawa. I only ended up staying there for one year before deciding to explore a different passion. My current goal is to gain practical experience in the electrical field while still working on software projects.

📚 I discorvered my love for book during my time in Ottawa. Living alone in the full force of the pandemic, I found new ways to keep me busy. The only exposure I had to reading before this was high school mandatory reading and I didn't enjoy it much. Now that I get to pick my own books, mainly sci-fi and fantasy, I've grown to love it.
